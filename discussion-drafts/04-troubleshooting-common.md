Post your issue here if you are not sure it's a bug yet.

Before posting:

- Clear caches (plugin cache, server cache, CDN)
- Try a minimal reproduction on a staging site
- Confirm builder version and WP version
- Disable conflicting plugins one by one (only on staging)

When you post, include:

- builder
- steps to reproduce
- screenshots (redacted)
